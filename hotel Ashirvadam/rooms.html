Room at Hotel Ashirvadam

At Hotel Ashirvadam, we provide well-appointed rooms that combine style and functionality.
Every room features modern amenities including air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi, LED TV,
premium bedding, 24-hour room service, and a clean, well-equipped bathroom.

Our rooms are divided into three categories:

Deluxe room

Perfect for: Solo travelers or couples looking for a cozy and affordable stay.

Executive room

Perfect for: Business travelers or small families who prefer added space and features.

Suite room

Perfect for: Guests who desire luxury, privacy, and personalized services.
